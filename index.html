Overview of the 6th Convocation of the eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre (EASTC)

Overview of the 6th Convocation of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre (EASTC)The 6 th convocation of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre of the year 2023 continues the tradition within academic institutions of gathering together academicians, students, alumni, benefactors, and industry professionals as stakeholders of the university community. If is the time to exchange views on topical issues that touch teaching. research, and professional development.
year's convocation has selected the topic "The role of official statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development of Tanzania" as the standing point to reflect on the theme of this year's 18 of November African Statistics Day. The theme states "Modernizing data ecosystems to accelerate the implementation of African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA): The role of official statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development of Africa"
